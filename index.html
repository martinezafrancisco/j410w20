Start Screen

Since its release in 1999, the Super Smash Bros. video game series has captivated millions of video gamers on various generations of Nintendo consoles. The series' most recent release, Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, is the second-highest selling game in the Nintendo Switch's history, with over 17 million copies sold as of 2019, according to Nintendo financial data.

The game is known for having a crossover of video game characters from different series — Super Mario Bros., Pokémon, The Legend of Zelda, to name a few — and its unique approach to the fighting game genre. Instead of depleting an opponent's health bar, players have to knock their opponents off the stage.

The Central Coast Smash scene sees players from various age groups, from high school students to adults, from and up and down the region — from Paso Robles to Santa Barbara. All of these players have one thing in common: their love for the game.
